## Link relationship notes

- `/workbench-buying-guide/` is an orphaned post -- it links out to
  /best-hand-planes/ but receives no inbound body links from any other page.
  Recommend adding a link from /best-hand-planes/ (tool guides cluster).

- `/scary-sharp-method/` has no inbound link from the sharpening pillar
  (/sharpening-guide/). It links to the pillar but the pillar does not link
  back. One-directional linking weakens cluster authority.

- `/why-hand-tools/` and `/choosing-wood-for-furniture/` are standalone
  articles with no inbound body links. They link out but are not linked to.
  Low cluster integration.

- Several posts use `http://benchcraftworkshop.com/...` internal links
  instead of `https://`. Confirmed on: /hand-plane-sharpening/ (link to
  /sharpening-guide/), /dovetail-joints-guide/ (link to /joinery-techniques/).
  These pass PageRank through a redirect but leak a small amount per hop.
